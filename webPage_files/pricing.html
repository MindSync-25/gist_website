Frequently asked questions

Can I use Gist without creating an account?

Yes — browse the public feed without signing up. Creating an account unlocks Voice, comments, personalisation, and your own profile.

How do AI comics work?

Our pipeline reads verified news stories each day, generates illustrated comic panels using AI image models, and publishes them in the app automatically. Editorial checks are run before anything goes live.

Can I cancel Pro anytime?

Absolutely. Cancel in the app with one tap — no hoops, no delays. You keep Pro access until the end of your billing period.

What is Voice, exactly?

Voice is Gist's civic debate feature. Each issue has three positions — Support, Oppose, or Question. You pick one, optionally leave a comment, and see live sentiment from readers across your city, state, or nation.
