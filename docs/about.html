✦ Our story

Built for the
conversations that matter.

We built Gist because social media wasn't built for the conversations people actually want to have. So we built something better.

Our Mission

Social media is broken. Not because there's too little content — because there's no real conversation. People scroll, react, and move on. Voices get buried. Opinions go unheard. Gist is built to fix that.

We're building a social platform where your take actually lands — where you can post, debate, connect, and discover people who think alongside you. Not a passive feed. A real social space for real people with real things to say.

Our Values

✦ Real people, real voices

Every post, take, and debate on Gist comes from a real person. We prioritise genuine expression over algorithmic amplification.

◈ Local first

What's happening in your city matters. Gist treats local voices as first-class — not an afterthought buried below national trends.

⊙ Conversation over consumption

We don't just want you to scroll. We want you to post, debate, reply, and connect. Social media should feel like a conversation — not a broadcast.

→ AI in service of people

Our AI helps surface the right content, illustrate complex ideas, and support real discussions. It does not replace human expression — it amplifies it.

The Team

Gist is built by a focused team of engineers, product designers, and people who care deeply about how communities connect online. We're small by choice — fast, opinionated, and obsessed with the product.
